BarPipal - Restaurant & Lodge | Try Once - Get addicted

Bar Pipal Restaurant & Lodge is a scenic spot by the Marsyangdi River near Akala Devi Temple. We serve fresh organic meals, offer rooms with scenic views, and provide a garden setting for relaxation and celebrations. From family stays to weddings and birthdays, Bar Pipal is where nature, tradition, and joy come together.

Why Bar Pipal?

More than just a restaurant — Bar Pipal is where great food, warm ambiance, and unforgettable evenings come together.

Authentic Taste

Fresh ingredients and carefully crafted recipes that bring rich and memorable flavors. Every thing is fresh and organic. Once you try it, you'll be addicted to us

Relaxing Atmosphere at Night

Beautiful lighting and a relaxing atmosphere perfect for friends and family gatherings.In the night the real beauty of Bar Pipal shines through.Cherry trees and its serene ambiance make it a perfect spot to unwind.

Perfect Location

Located in the heart of Tanahun, near the scenic Marsyangdi River and Akala Devi Temple. When Tradition meets Nature and Taste then its Bar Pipal!
